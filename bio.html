TANJILA SUMAIA SIDDIQYE

BIO

Background

In 1991, She has born in Mymensingh, Bangladesh. Tanjila Post-graduated from Faculty of Fine Arts University of Dhaka in Ceramic. Her studies included studio classes in sculpture, drawing, design, Art history along with various workshop like Thanka Painting in NEPAL, Terracotta Plaque in Dhaka university, Terracotta Musk in Trimatra, Ceramic workshop in Vertical, Acrylic painting and Water color painting in Srijon bikash art academy. Presently she is running her own Art organization called “Artopolitan” to create awareness about art among common people. Organized 6th International art exhibition in Bangladesh, India and United Kingdom. So that the idea about art becomes higher in society.
